Know Before You Go

Tips for your best goat yoga experience

🧘‍♀️

Wear Comfy Layers

Choose yoga gear that's comfortable and that you don't mind getting a little "farm-dusty." Goats love zippers and drawstrings!

🚿

Mat Cleaning Provided

We provide organic, goat-safe mat cleaner for use after every session to keep your gear fresh and clean.

📸

Photo-Op Time

The last 15 minutes of every class are dedicated to goat cuddles and photos. We'll help you get that perfect selfie!
